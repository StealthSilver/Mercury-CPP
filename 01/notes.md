## üîπ FLOWCHARTS

Flowcharts are **logical diagrams** used to represent the solution of a problem.  
They help in understanding the **step-by-step flow of logic** before writing actual code.

---

## üîπ ELEMENTS OF A FLOWCHART

1. **Oval** ‚Äì Start or Exit
2. **Parallelogram** ‚Äì Input or Output
3. **Rectangle** ‚Äì Process
4. **Rhombus (Diamond)** ‚Äì Decision
5. **Arrows** ‚Äì Direction of Flow
